Mathematical Modelling and Data Science at OsloMet

About the Program

Mathematical Modelling at OsloMet integrates mathematics, data science, and computational techniques to address real-world challenges. The program spans three years, totaling 180 ECTS credits, and is conducted in Norwegian and English.

The first year covers foundational topics such as mathematics, physics, and programming. The final semester includes a practical bachelor's thesis linked to industry challenges.

Career Opportunities

Graduates are equipped to work in:

  • Data Science and AI
  • Engineering Applications
  • Finance and Risk Analysis
  • Research and Development

Program Structure

Program Overview
Year Semester Key Subjects
1 1 Discrete Mathematics, Programming, Technology & Society
1 2 Mathematics 1000, Physics & Chemistry, Databases
2 3 Modern Physics, Mathematics 2000, Statistical Methods
2 4 Algorithms & Data Structures, Advanced Modelling
3 5 Machine Learning, Data Science Projects
3 6 Bachelor Thesis

Admission Requirements

  • General University Admission Certification
  • Mathematics R1 and R2
  • Physics 1
  • Alternative pathways include completing a one-year preparatory course for engineering studies.
